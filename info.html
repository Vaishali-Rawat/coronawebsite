    HOW Coronavirus came into the Nation

    The COVID-19 pandemic in India is part of the worldwide pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first case of COVID-19 in India, which originated from China, was reported on 30 January 2020.The first cases of COVID-19 in India were reported in the towns of Thrissur, Alappuzha and Kasargod, all in the state of Kerala, among three Indian medical students who had returned from Wuhan. Lockdowns were announced in Kerala on 23 March, and in the rest of the country on 25 March. By mid-May 2020, five cities accounted for around half of all reported cases in the country: Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, Chennai and Thane. On 10 June, India's recoveries exceeded active cases for the first time. Infection rates started to drop in September, along with the number of new and active cases.

    Symptoms of Coronavirus

    1. Sore throat
    2. Headache
    3. Chills, sometimes with shaking
    4. Loss of smell or taste
    5. Congestion or runny nose
    6. Nausea or vomiting
    7. Diarrhea

    How to Prevent it ?

    1. Wash your hands well and often. Use hand sanitizer when you’re not near soap and water.
    2. Try not to touch your face.
    3. Wear a face mask when you go out.
    4. Follow your community guidelines for staying home.
    5. When you do go out in public, leave at least 6 feet of space between you and others.
    6. Don’t leave your house if you don’t feel well.
    7. Cover your mouth with your elbow when you cough or sneeze, or use a tissue.
    8. Clean and disinfect places you come in touch with.
